Privacy Policy for Laptop Battery Widget

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

Introduction

Laptop Battery Widget ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Device Pairing Information: When you pair your iPhone with your laptop, we collect and store:
    • A unique device identifier
    • Server connection details
    • Authentication credentials (secret keys)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Battery Status Data: We collect your laptop's battery information including:
    • Battery percentage
    • Charging status
    • Last update timestamp
  • Push Notification Token: Your Apple Push Notification Service (APNS) token to enable real-time battery updates
  • Debug Logs (Optional): If you enable Developer Mode, diagnostic information about app updates and refresh events

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Display your laptop's battery status on your iPhone
  • Send push notifications with battery updates
  • Update the home screen widget with current battery information
  • Enable background refresh functionality
  • Provide debugging information when Developer Mode is enabled

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All battery data and pairing credentials are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is stored in an App Group container shared between the main app and widget extension
  • No battery status data is stored on external servers beyond temporary caching

Server Communication

  • The app communicates with a server you specify during pairing (typically running on your laptop)
  • All server communications use authentication headers to verify identity
  • Connection details are encrypted using iOS's standard security features

Data Retention

  • Battery data is retained only as long as you remain paired with your laptop
  • When you disconnect your device through the app settings, all pairing data and battery history are deleted
  • Debug logs (if enabled) retain up to 50 recent update events

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties.

The app operates as follows:

  • Battery data flows only between your laptop and your iPhone
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to your data
  • No data is transmitted to third parties beyond standard Apple services (APNS)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect: Remove the pairing between your laptop and iPhone at any time through app settings
  • Delete Data: All local data is deleted when you disconnect your device
  • Control Notifications: Manage push notification permissions through iOS Settings
  • Control Background Refresh: Enable or disable background app refresh through iOS Settings
  • Access Debug Information: View update logs when Developer Mode is enabled

Children's Privacy

Laptop Battery Widget is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

The app uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNS): To deliver battery status updates
  • WidgetKit: To display battery information on your home screen
  • Background Tasks: To refresh battery data when the app is not active

Apple's use of your information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, available at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

QR Code Scanning

The app uses device camera access solely for scanning QR codes during the pairing process. Camera data is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notice in the app when significant changes occur

Your continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source

This app's source code may be made available under an open-source license. Any use of the code is subject to the terms of that license and this Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Secure authentication using secret keys
  • Local encryption of sensitive data using iOS security features
  • Secure HTTPS communication with your laptop server
  • No permanent storage of credentials on remote servers

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Users

The app is designed for personal use and operates through a direct connection between your devices. Data does not leave your personal network unless you configure your laptop server to be accessible externally.
